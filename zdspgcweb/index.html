About ZDSPGC

Providing quality, relevant, and affordable education for the youth of Zamboanga del Sur since 1998.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality, and relevant education that nurtures competent, ethical, and service-oriented professionals who contribute to national and global development.

Our Vision

A leading provincial college recognized for excellence in instruction, research, and community extension services.

Read Our History

Why Choose ZDSPGC?

  • Affordable tuition with scholarship opportunities
  • Modern facilities and updated curriculum
  • Industry-experienced faculty members
  • Strong industry partnerships for employment
  • Vibrant campus life and student organizations
